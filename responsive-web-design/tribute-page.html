Mustafa Kemal ATATÜRK

Here's a timeline of Mustafa Kemal ATATÜRK's life:

  • 1881 - Born in Salonica, Ottoman Empire
  • 1893 - Mustafa enters military preparatory school in Salonica.
  • 1899 - Mustafa Kemal enters infantry class of War College in Konstantiniyye (now known as İstanbul).
  • 1902 - Commissioned Second Lieutenant, and enters Staff College.
  • 1903 - Promoted First Lieutenant.
  • 1905 - Passes out as Staff Captain and is posted for Fifth Army in Syria; revives a secret opposition group in Damascus.
  • 1907 - Promoted Adjutant-Major.
  • 1907 - Posted to Third Army headquarters in Salonica.
  • 1908 - Mustafa Kemal travels to Tripoli and Benghazi to re-establish CUP.
  • 1909 - Appointed chief of staff of 7th reserve division in Salonica.
  • 1909 - Travels with his division to the outskirts of Konstantiniyye; 31 March Incident.
  • 1910 - Takes part in suppress of Albanian revolt.
  • 1911 - Appointed to 5th army corps headquarters.
  • 1911 - Commander 38th infantry regiment.
  • 1911 - Posted to general staff in Konstantiniyye.
  • 1911 - Volunteers for service against the Italians in Cyrenaica.
  • 1911 - Promoted Major.
  • 1911 - Appointed commander of Derne sector in Cyrenaica; Italo-Turkish War.
  • 1912 - Mustafa Kemal leaves Cyrenaica and returns to Konstantiniyye.
  • 1912 - Appointed director of operations of Straits Composite Force.
  • 1913 - Mustafa Kemal appointed military attaché in Sofia.
  • 1914 - Mustafa Kemal promoted Lieutenant-Colonel.
  • 1915 - Mustafa Kemal leaves Sofia to take up appointment as commander of 19th division for service in Battle of Gallipoli.
  • 1915 - Defeats Allied troops Landing at Anzac Cove.
  • 1915 - Defeats the British at Battle of Sari Bair.
  • 1919 - Appointed the Inspector of the Ninth Army Troops.
  • 1919 - Mustafa Kemal lands in Samsun.
  • 1919 - Mustafa Kemal resigns from the post of Inspector of Third Army and from the army.
  • 1919 - Mustafa Kemal elected Chairman of Erzurum Congress.
  • 1920 - Mustafa Kemal opens the Grand National Assembly (BMM) in Angora (now Ankara).
  • 1920 - Mustafa Kemal is condemned to death by the government in Konstantiniyye.
  • 1921 - Mustafa Appointed Commander - in - Chief by the Grand National Assembly.
  • 1921 - The battle of Sakarya begins with Turkish troops led by Mustafa Kemal.
  • 1921 - Mustafa Kemal receives the rank of Marshal and the title Gazi.
  • 1922 - Mustafa Gazi Mustafa Kemal begins to lead Great Offensive from the hill of Kocatepe.
  • 1922 - Mustafa Mustafa Kemal at the Battle of Dumlupınar.
  • 1922 - Enters Izmir.
  • 1923 - Mustafa Mustafa Kemal and Latife Uşşakizade marry in Izmir.
  • 1923 - Elected first president.
  • 1934 - The Grand National Assembly of Turkey passes a law granting him the surname "ATATÜRK".
  • 1938 - Mustafa Kemal died at age 58.

Peace at Home, Peace in the World
